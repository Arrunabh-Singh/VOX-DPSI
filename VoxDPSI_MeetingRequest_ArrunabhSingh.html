Student Grievance Management System
The Problem

At DPS Indore, student concerns disappear into verbal loops — passed from person to person with no record, no accountability, and no resolution timeline. Students stay silent because there is no safe, structured way to be heard.

The Inspiration

Inspired by Indore's award-winning 311 civic grievance system — India's model for transparent public accountability — Vox DPSI brings the same principle inside school walls: every concern gets a tracking number, a handler, and a timeline.

Vox DPSI is a full-stack student grievance management platform built by the Student Council of Delhi Public School Indore. Students raise complaints with a unique tracking number (VOX-001 format), which are assigned to council members, escalated through teachers and coordinators with complete audit trails, and resolved with SLA timers enforcing accountability at every step. Anonymity controls, WhatsApp notifications, and an appeals system ensure both safety and fairness.

Key Features
🔒
Anonymity Control
Students request anonymity. Council always sees the name. Identity revealed to teachers and coordinators only with explicit consent — logged in the audit trail.
⏱️
SLA Timers
Every complaint has a 48-hour resolution window. On Track → Due Soon → Act Now → OVERDUE — visible to all handlers in real time.
📊
Analytics Dashboard
Domain breakdowns, section heatmaps, resolution rates, council performance scores, and 7-day complaint volume timeline — all in one view.
💬
WhatsApp Notifications
Instant WhatsApp alerts via Twilio when complaints are assigned, escalated, or resolved. No one can claim they did not know.
✅
In-Person Verification
Council members must mark complaints Verified after meeting the student face-to-face. No digital shortcuts — accountability built into the workflow.
📜
Full Audit Trail
Every action — escalation, vote, note, status change — is timestamped and logged in a tamper-evident timeline. Complete institutional memory.
